How It Works — 3 Steps
① Hit the minimum threshold
Every role has a qualifying floor. Below it, no commission is earned. This ensures the scheme rewards genuine performance only.
② Earn at the standard rate
Once threshold is cleared, every unit (booking, query, saving) earns at the base rate. These are fixed and transparent.
③ Accelerate past the target
Hitting stretch targets unlocks multipliers. Focus destinations earn higher per-booking rates.
Key Rules
Paid 1 Month in Arrears
Commission for April is paid on 5th May. This allows Nexus data to be verified and validated before payout.
Cancellation Rule (Sales)
Cancelled bookings don't count. Cancellation rate above 5% reduces the qualifying booking count proportionally.
Override is All-or-Nothing
Team Leaders and Managers only earn their override if their team/dept hits the threshold. No partial payment.

👑 Leadership Override Calculator
Team Leaders earn 15% override on team commission. Managers earn 25% on department commission. Both require hitting team/dept threshold.
How Override Commission Works
?
Override CommissionYour override is a % of what your team/department earns — not a separate metric. The better your team performs, the more you earn. This directly incentivises you to coach, develop, and support your people.
Team Leader — 15% override
Applied to the total commission earned by your team this month. Only unlocks if team achieves ≥80% of monthly target. All or nothing.
Manager — 25% override
Applied to the total commission earned by your whole department. Unlocks only if department achieves ≥85% of target.
⚠️ All-or-nothing threshold
No partial overrides. Miss the 80%/85% threshold by even 1% and zero override is paid. This protects the scheme from being gamed.
