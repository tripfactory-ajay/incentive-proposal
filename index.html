📌 How It Works
Step 1 — Hit the minimum
Every department has a qualifying threshold. No commission is earned below this level.
Step 2 — Earn base commission
Standard rates apply once threshold is met. These are fixed per booking/query/saving.
Step 3 — Accelerate
Stretch goals unlock higher rates and multipliers. Destination bonuses stack on top.
⚠️ Key Rules
Cancellation Rate
Sales must maintain <5% cancellation rate. Excess cancellations reduce qualifying booking count.
Team Leader Override
TLs only earn their 15% override if their team achieves at least 80% of monthly target.
Payment Date
All commissions paid on the 5th of the following month. One month in arrears always.
📡 Tracking System
All performance data is tracked and verified through the Nexus DMC Reservation System. Commission calculations are based solely on data recorded in Nexus — no manual submissions are accepted.
What Nexus Tracks
Bookings, cancellations, destination codes, query resolution, cost savings, tour confirmations, and client satisfaction scores.
Disputes
Any commission queries must be raised with your line manager by the 20th of the following month.
